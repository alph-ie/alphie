## 👩‍💻 About Me

Hello! I'm a **Full Stack Developer**, **Virtual Assistant**, and **Cyber Security PKI Engineer** passionate about creating secure, efficient, and user-friendly digital solutions.

<details>
<summary><strong>📖 My Journey in Tech</strong></summary>
<br/>

I studied at **Arba Minch University**, where I developed a unique skill set that bridges the gap between frontend aesthetics, backend functionality, and enterprise-grade security.

My journey took an exciting turn during my internship at **INSA (Information Network Security Agency)**, where I worked in the **PKI Domain Operations and Development** domain.
